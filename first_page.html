Why I Started This Blog

For as long as I can remember, I've loved coding and computers. Learning new things about technology has always been one of my favorite hobbies. The problem is that I often struggle to stay focused for long periods of time. So I thought: why not start a blog where I share what I've learned? Maybe writing about my progress will keep me motivated and help me stay consistent. We'll see. At the very least, it's worth a try.

I can learn HTML CSS too!
